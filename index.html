About RidePool

RidePool is a car pooling platform that connects passengers with drivers who are traveling on the same route. Our goal is to reduce travel costs, traffic congestion, and carbon emissions by enabling shared rides.

How Payments Work

Passengers make payments securely through our platform using Razorpay. The payment is collected by RidePool and later settled to the respective driver after the successful completion of the ride.

RidePool may charge a small service fee for facilitating the ride and payment.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • If the driver cancels the ride, passengers receive a 100% refund.
  • If a passenger cancels before the ride start time, a full or partial refund may be issued based on cancellation timing.
  • No refunds are provided for completed rides.
  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

Privacy Policy

We respect user privacy. Personal information such as name, contact details, and payment information is collected only to provide our services and is never sold to third parties.

All payments are processed securely via Razorpay. RidePool does not store sensitive card or UPI details.
