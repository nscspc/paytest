About RidePool

RidePool is a car pooling platform that connects passengers with drivers who are traveling on the same route. The platform enables shared rides to reduce travel costs, traffic congestion, and environmental impact.

Pricing (All charges in INR ₹)

  • Platform service fee: ₹15 per ride
  • Distance charge: ₹10 per km (paid to driver)
  • Cancellation fee: ₹20 (if cancelled after driver acceptance)

The total ride cost is calculated before booking and shown to the passenger prior to payment.

Payments

All payments on RidePool are processed securely using Razorpay in Indian Rupees (INR). Passengers can pay using UPI, cards, net banking, and other supported payment methods.

RidePool collects the payment on behalf of the driver and settles the ride amount to the driver after successful ride completion.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • If the driver cancels the ride, the passenger receives a 100% refund.
  • If the passenger cancels before the ride start time, a full or partial refund may be issued based on cancellation timing.
  • No refunds are applicable once the ride is successfully completed.
  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

Privacy Policy

RidePool respects user privacy. Personal information such as name, contact details, and ride information is collected only for service delivery.

Payment details are handled securely by Razorpay. RidePool does not store sensitive card or UPI information.
